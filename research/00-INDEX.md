# Bharatiya Antariksh Hackathon 2026 (BAH 2026)

National hackathon by **ISRO**, powered by **Hack2skill**. Third edition.
Source page: https://hack2skill.com/event/bah2026

## Key facts
- **Eligibility:** Students only (UG / PG / PhD / research scholars), enrolled in an Indian institution. No working professionals. India only.
- **Team size:** 3–4 members (can be from different colleges).
- **Cost:** Free.
- **This phase (now):** Idea / concept proposal only — **no prototype required** for the July 1 submission (FAQ #6).
- **Reward:** Recognition by ISRO + possible ISRO internship + II-AC travel reimbursement for finalists. No cash prize listed.

## General submission rules (Video 2 Q&A)
- **Idea phase = a PPT/idea-deck filled into the mandatory template** on the dashboard (Submissions → Idea Submission Phase → download template, fill, upload, submit). Contains: problem statement selected, idea brief, technology stack, idea deck. **Closes 1 July.**
- **Your idea gets you shortlisted; your prototype (built during the 30-hour finale) wins.** No prototype needed now.
- Judges **prefer innovative over merely practical/scalable** solutions ("we can scale later; innovation is what we're looking for").
